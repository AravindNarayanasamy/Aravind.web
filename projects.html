Multi Share :

developed an application called Multi-Share to send and receive files unlike other application this application can send and receive a single file to multiple users at a same time ,it doesn’t require the sender to individually connect to the hotspot of the first receiver during the first transfer, during the second transfer the sender to the hotspot of the second user.

developed this application using blockcode which is predefined already by the developers.we can easily place the block by just drag and droping.

All these transfer can be only shared and received to those who have Android version of 10 or below 10.
